## Paleo Pancakes

Paleo Pancakes, makes ~8 5in pancakes

### Ingredients

#### Dry
1. 80g Almond Flour
2. 40g Tapioca Flour
3. 4g Salt
4. 6g Baking Powder

#### Wet
1. 40g Melted Butter
2. 80g Milk
3. 30g Maple Syrup
4. 2 Eggs
5. 4g Vanilla Extract


### Steps

1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Mix wet ingredients
3. Combine, but don't overbeat
4. Make pancakes over medium heat, best if <5" in diameter
